About

Lilly's Collective celebrates the timeless art of handloom weaving and handcrafted designs. Inspired by the soothing rhythm of hand-woven threads on pristine white cloth, each creation is a testament to tradition, patience, and artistry. Discover handmade patterns—every thread narrates a unique story, woven with care.

Founder

Hi, I’m Keerthi – a student and a beginner in embroidery, stepping into the world of creativity with hope and passion. I recently discovered that creativity is not just a hobby but my true calling. I’m weaving it into my dream of building a unique creative business where imagination meets reality. Every stitch I make carries a piece of joy, and my wish is simple – to bring a mesmerizing smile to your face through my designs. This journey is very close to my heart, and I would love your support as I grow, learn, and work towards fulfilling my dream.
